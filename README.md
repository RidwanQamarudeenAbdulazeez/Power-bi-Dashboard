# Power-bi-Dashboard
A multi-page Power BI dashboard built to analyze and visualize sales data. It includes KPIs, product insights, customer breakdowns, and performance trends in an interactive and user-friendly format.
## Dataset Used
<a href="https://github.com/RidwanQamarudeenAbdulazeez/Power-bi-Dashboard/blob/main/Retail%20Sales%20Analysis%20Project%202025.xlsx">Dataset</a>
##Questions (KPIs)
The dashboard answers the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
#Primary KPIs
Total Sales: This KPI shows the overall amount of money generated from all sales within the selected period.
Total Profit: This KPI shows the money the business actually gained after subtracting all costs from total sales.
Average Order Value (AOV): This KPI measures the average amount of money a customer spends each time they place an order.
Profit Margin: This KPI shows the percentage of sales that becomes profit, indicating how efficiently the business is making money from its revenue.
#Analytical Questions Answered
Which customer segment contributes the highest sales?
Which product categories and products generate the most revenue?
What is the sales trend across quarters?
Which customers are the top revenue generators?
Which regions or states perform better in sales?
What is the sales distribution across product categories?

##Process
1. Data Import: Loaded the dataset into Power BI.
2. Data Cleaning: Fixed missing values, corrected data types, removed duplicates.
3. Data Modeling: Created relationships and DAX measures for KPIs.
4. Visualization: Built dashboard pages for overview, customers, products and region.
5. Insight Extraction: Interpreted visuals for business insights.
